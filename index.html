Project Overview

Bridging Frontier Research & Higher Education

This project unites institutions in the UK and Vietnam to embed frontier research in Digital Twins, Next-Generation Wireless Networks, and Quantum Technologies into higher education curricula.

By developing early-career lecturers' capacity and enhancing students' access to research-informed learning, we aim to strengthen institutional capability to deliver inclusive, internationally competitive ICT programmes aligned with Vietnam's strategic technological priorities.

Project Roadmap

Key Project Objectives

Our roadmap to integrating advanced research into practical education.

Research-Informed Programmes

Jointly design pilot short courses on Vietnam's prioritised technologies, embedding frontier research on Digital Twins, IoT, and Cloud Computing.

Research-to-Teaching Integration

Develop proposals, convert findings into case studies, and co-supervise students in advanced ICT topics.

Knowledge Exchange

Host workshops and seminars to demonstrate research-to-teaching integration and present outputs at ICT conferences.

Academic Outputs

Publish high-impact research and create a specialised 'Research-to-Teaching Toolkit' for lecturers.

Student Employability

Introduce online-placement opportunities with UK companies, enabling Vietnamese ICT students to gain international industry exposure.

Activities

Project Timeline (2026-2027)

Feb - May 2026

Webinar Series on Emerging Tech

6-hour series co-hosted by MDX and VNUHCM-UIT. Topics include Digital Twins, Next-Gen Wireless, and Quantum Computing. Targeted at students, early-career researchers, and industry.

Summer 2026

Partner Visits & Joint Planning

Reciprocal visits to develop new taught programmes and draft research proposals. Includes visits to industrial partners.

Jul - Sep 2026

Pilot Joint Short Courses

Co-designed short courses on Digital Twins, IoT, and 6G. Providing background on contemporary research and certification for students.

Dec 2026

Researcher Training

Online training for PhD candidates and early-career researchers on research methods, publication skills, and grant writing.

Early 2027

Industry-Linked Online Placements

Remote placements with UK companies in software development and data analytics to enhance student employability.
